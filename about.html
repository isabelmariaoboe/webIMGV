I am a Front-End Development student at the University of South-Eastern Norway.

I have Masters in Music Performance and I have been always interested in the use of new technologies in the fields of music and teaching. Digital resources and Internet have provided us with great tools to benefit from.

For me, everything started creating content for the YouTube channel: Den Magiske Skogen for Obo. New challenges, the desire of creating attractive designs and the chance to make others routine easier made me focus on improving my digital skills.

As you could probably see on my works, they all have very different styles. I am enjoying my growth in this path, trying new things but most importantly, being able to visualize the client's ideas and create them.
